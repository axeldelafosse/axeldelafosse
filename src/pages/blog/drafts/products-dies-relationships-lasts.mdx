# Products dies, relationships lasts

## Heard this quote from Ashton Kutcher during a YC alumni Q&A.

Inspired by Ashton and a post on Bookface from my S17 batchmates Gamelynx, I wanted to write this to remind me of something.

Your product can die. Your company can fail. But relationships can lasts.

Even during bad times, keep your head up. Treat your co-founders well. Make sure to respect and admire each other. Listen to each other.

[Be earnest and honest](/blog/the-importance-of-being-earnest). It's OK to ask for help. It's OK to [stop and accept the failure](https://avc.com/2018/11/pivot-or-fail/). Don't fear your investors, they're here to help.

> “Even if you aren't giving up long term, don't be afraid to withdraw and reassess. Win the way you want to win, not the way you feel you're obligated to.” — [Alexander Mistakidis](https://amist.co)

If your products dies, it's alright. Take care of these relationships!
